About Fairouz

Fairouz, born Nouhad Haddad, is a Lebanese singer widely regarded as one of the greatest voices in the history of Arab music. Her career began in the 1950s, and over decades she built an extraordinary musical legacy that weaves together Lebanese folk tradition, classical melody, and deeply emotional songs.

She collaborated primarily with the Rahbani Brothers, producing musicals and timeless works that became part of the collective Arab memory.

To this day, Fairouz's songs fill quiet mornings and family evenings, continuing to find their way into the hearts of new listeners around the world.
